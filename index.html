Detect Fire & Smoke — Real-Time

Upload recorded clips or use live camera to detect fire & smoke using a trained deep-learning model (YOLO/CNN). Annotated output videos with bounding boxes are produced.

Fast

Near real-time processing and alerts.

Accurate

Deep learning with frame-level analysis.

Portable

Works with webcams and recorded video files.

Live Demo
Upload a short clip and view annotated output

About the System

The backend uses OpenCV and YOLO/CNN models to analyze frames and detect fire or smoke. Detections are localized with bounding boxes and timestamps returned to the frontend for display.

Model

YOLOv8 / custom CNN

Libraries

OpenCV, NumPy, PyTorch/TensorFlow

Backend

Flask or FastAPI
